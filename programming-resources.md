My personal favorites collected over the years. Most of the topics revolve
around general computing, "low-level" or graphics programming, and game
development; but not only. You might also want to take a look at
[build-your-own-x](https://github.com/codecrafters-io/build-your-own-x).

## General Computing

- [From NAND to Tetris](https://www.nand2tetris.org/) (Shimon Schocken &amp; Noam Nisan)
- [The Pattern on the Stone](https://duckduckgo.com/?q=isbn:9780465066933) (W. Daniel Hillis)
- [Build a 6502 Computer](https://eater.net/6502) (Ben Eater)
- [Handmade Hero](https://handmadehero.org/) ([Youtube Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLnuhp3Xd9PYTt6svyQPyRO_AAuMWGxPzU)) (Casey Muratori)
- [Computer, Enhance!](https://www.computerenhance.com/) (Casey Muratori)
- [Write Your Own Virtual Machine](https://www.jmeiners.com/lc3-vm/) (Justin Meiners &amp; Ryan Pendleton)

## C

- [21st Century C](https://duckduckgo.com/?q=isbn:9781491903896) (Ben Klemens): The only C book I found helpful if you already know programming.
- [nullprogram.com](https://nullprogram.com) (Chris Wellons):
    - [My favorite C compiler flags during development](https://nullprogram.com/blog/2023/04/29/)
    - [My review of the C standard library in practice](https://nullprogram.com/blog/2023/02/11/)

## Memory Management

- [Memory Allocation](https://samwho.dev/memory-allocation/) (Sam Rose)
- [Memory Allocation Strategies](https://www.gingerbill.org/series/memory-allocation-strategies/)  (Ginger Bill)
 - [A Fixed Block Memory Allocator in C](https://github.com/endurodave/C_Allocator) (David Lafreniere)

## Graphics Programming

- [How OpenGL works: software rendering in 500 lines of code](https://github.com/ssloy/tinyrenderer/wiki/Lesson-0:-getting-started) (Dmitry V. Sokolov)
- [Understandable RayTracing in 256 lines of bare C++](https://github.com/ssloy/tinyraytracer/wiki/Part-1:-understandable-raytracing) (Dmitry V. Sokolov)
- [Ray Tracing in One Weekend](https://raytracing.github.io/books/RayTracingInOneWeekend.html) (Peter Shirley)
- [486 lines of C++: old-school FPS in a weekend](https://github.com/ssloy/tinyraycaster/wiki/Part-0:-getting-started) (Dmitry V. Sokolov)
- [KABOOM! in 180 lines of bare C++](https://github.com/ssloy/tinykaboom/wiki/KABOOM!-in-180-lines-of-code) (Dmitry V. Sokolov)
- [The Book of Shaders](https://thebookofshaders.com) (Patricio Gonzalez Vivo &amp; Jen Lowe)
- [SDL2 common mistakes and how to avoid them](https://nullprogram.com/blog/2023/01/08/) (Chris Wellons)
